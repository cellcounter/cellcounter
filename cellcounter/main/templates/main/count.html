Welcome to Cellcountr

To get started, click the red button to start performing a count.

  • Press the key corresponding to a cell type to add a count.
  • To remove a count press 'Backspace+Key'.
  • To flag a cell as abnormal press 'Space+Key'.
  • To see example images of the cells in question press 'Shift+Key'.
  • Once your count is complete, click 'Close Keyboard' to see a tabulated report, and to download as a .csv

To see a tabulated differential report close the keyboard. You may also save the report to your local disk.
